# Welcome to Lumina

Lumina is a friendly place to track your food, health, and family. Here's the quick tour:

- **Today** — your dashboard. Everything you logged today at a glance.
- **Food** — log meals, build recipes, and review your diary over time.
- **Health** — vitals, sleep, fitness, and medical records.
- **More** — family sharing, AI settings, and this Help page.

Turn on **Learn Mode** (the 💡 in the top bar) anytime you see a symbol you don't recognize.
