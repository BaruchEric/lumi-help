# Logging a meal

1. From **Food**, tap **Quick Log** or the big **+** button on the Diary.
2. Type or speak what you ate ("2 eggs and coffee"). Lumina's AI fills in nutrition.
3. Review the nutrient pills — carbs, protein, fat, calories. Tap any for details.
4. Hit **Save**. The meal lands on today's diary.

**Tip:** long-press any nutrient pill to see what it means.
